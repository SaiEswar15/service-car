Our Services

Premium Automotive Services Designed to Exceed Your Expectations

Water Washing

Basic cleaning process that involves using high-pressure water to remove dirt, grime, and debris from the exterior of your vehicle. This is typically the first step in any car washing routine.

Foam Washing

Thorough cleaning method by applying a specially formulated foam to the surface of your vehicle before rinsing it off with high-pressure water and helps loosen dirt and grime, making it easier to remove.

Shampooing

Applying a concentrated cleaning solution to the surface and scrubbing it with a soft brush or mitt, effective at removing stubborn stains, oxidation, and other contaminants embedded in the paint.

Vaccuming & Detailing

Interior vacuuming and cleaning involve a thorough cleaning of the interior by vacuuming up dirt and debris from the seats, and floor mats, center console, and door panels, and wiping down all interior surfaces.

Dashboard & Carpet Cleaning

specialized cleaning services that focus on specific areas of your vehicle's interior. It helps helps to restore the shine and protect the surface.

Tyre Shining

Enhancing the appearance of your vehicle's tires by applying products designed to restore their luster, repel dirt and grime which only makes your tires look new and clean to maintain health and longevity.

Underbody Wash

Important for vehicles that are driven in harsh conditions, such as off-road or in rainy areas. Underbody washing can help to prevent rust and corrosion, which can lead to costly repairs.

Chassis Painting

Applying a protective coating to the underside of your vehicle to extend the life of your vehicle's chassis, often recommended for vehicles that are driven in harsh conditions or that are exposed to salt or other corrosive elements.

Teflon Polishing

Protective coating that can be applied to the exterior of your vehicle to help repel water, dirt, and grime. This coating protects your vehicle's paint from UV rays and other environmental factors.
